Hidden taxes

Time you don't even notice losing

The morning routine is just the beginning. Glasses and contacts steal time from every corner of your life.

🌧

Weather tax

Fogged glasses in the cold. Rain on your lenses. Stepping out of the sauna blind. Steam in the kitchen. Every single day.

🏊

Spontaneity tax

Can't jump in the pool. Can't play football on a whim. Every activity needs "vision prep" — lenses out, glasses on, glasses off.

✈️

Travel tax

Lens solution, spare glasses, hunting for a pharmacy abroad. Liquids limit at the airport. Always something extra to pack.

🏃

Sports tax

Sports goggles, fogged lenses at the gym, contacts out before swimming. Fear of losing your glasses. Every workout is harder than it needs to be.

🚨

Safety tax

3 AM, the smoke alarm goes off. Your first instinct: find your glasses. In an emergency, not being able to see makes you vulnerable.
